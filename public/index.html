Digital Literacy Module · Week 5 · Safe Browsing Practices

Welcome to the Northwind digital literacy review. In this session we cover responsible technology usage, campus device etiquette, and approved research tools. All students must read the entire module before accessing the district search portal.

1. Only approved engines should be used for classroom queries (Google, Britannica School, and Gale In Context). Any deviation requires teacher permission.

2. Tabs should remain on-topic and visible during instruction. Use of distractions, unapproved games, or messaging utilities is strictly prohibited.

3. When completing research, remember to cite primary and secondary sources following MLA 9th edition guidelines. Use district-provided citation tools where possible.

4. Report phishing attempts, suspicious downloads, or unexpected pop-ups to the technology office immediately. Safety drills are conducted monthly.

5. Keep your Chromebook charged, transport it in the protective sleeve, and avoid leaving it unattended in common areas.
