1. Remember the vertex form: y = a(x - h)^2 + k. Identify h and k to find the parabola’s axis of symmetry. Sketch on scrap paper.

2. Discriminant check: b^2 - 4ac. >0 means two solutions, 0 means one, <0 means complex. Use this to sanity-check calculator results.

3. Keep your workspace on-task. Arcadia devices are monitored for academic integrity. Close all recreational tabs before continuing.

4. When researching math topics online, rely on district-approved resources (Arcadia Math Studio, Khan-style practice sets, Desmos classroom activities).

5. Protect your password, use two-factor authentication, and report any suspicious messages to the digital safety team.
